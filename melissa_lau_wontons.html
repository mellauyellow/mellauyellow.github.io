Wontons

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Wontons are Chinese dumplings made of pork and shrimp.

More info on wontons from Wikipedia

Ingredients

  • pork
  • shrimp
  • wonton skins

Directions
  1. Mix pork and shrimp together in a bowl.
  2. Wrap pork/shrimp filling in wonton skins.
  3. Cook in boiling water for 5 minutes, or until floating at surface.
